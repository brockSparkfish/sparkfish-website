About Sparkfish

Real retail experience. Real infrastructure. Real commitment to the brands we partner with.

Our story

30+ years of retail, built into everything we do

Sparkfish is a family-owned and operated e-commerce company based in Lehi, Utah. Our roots in retail go back more than three decades — spanning brick and mortar storefronts and the full evolution of e-commerce — giving us a depth of experience that purely digital businesses simply don't have.

We built Sparkfish on a straightforward belief: that great selling starts with great product, and great product comes from manufacturers and distributors who stand behind what they make. Our job is to find those companies, buy their product, and put it in front of the right customers through the right storefronts.

Our family values drive everything we do. We want to find the best deal on the best product and deliver the best customer experience we can. We work hard to present our customers with as many opportunities and unique product lines as possible — partnering with quality brands from across the country to bring products that companies genuinely stand behind.
